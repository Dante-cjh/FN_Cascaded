- reason: A short paragraph explaining your final judgment, noting whether you agree or disagree with the small model and why.

{source_text}

[REPRESENTATIVE REPLIES]
{selected_replies}

[PROPAGATION STATS]
reply_count={num_replies}, max_depth={max_depth}, num_branches={num_branches}

[SMALL MODEL REPORT]
prediction: {small_pred}
